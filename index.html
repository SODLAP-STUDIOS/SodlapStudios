About Sodlap Studio

Sodlap Studios was founded by 6 students who wanted to make a game that solves an issue, the issue being the lack of Filipino folklore RPG games. Our team developed this game with the vision of bringing Filipino folklore into the world of modern gaming. We wanted to create more than just a source of entertainment — we wanted to build an experience that reflects the rich stories, beliefs, and traditions of the Philippines. As we explored different games and genres, we noticed that Filipino myths and legends are rarely represented in the gaming industry. While many cultures are widely featured in games, the Philippines has countless untold stories and legendary creatures that deserve recognition. This inspired us to create a game that gives life to these forgotten tales and proudly represents Filipino culture. The purpose of this game is to spread awareness of Philippine folklore to both local and international audiences. Through immersive storytelling, characters, and environments inspired by Filipino legends, we hope players can discover and appreciate the uniqueness of our culture. By sharing these stories through gaming, we aim to preserve Filipino heritage while introducing it to a new generation of players around the world.

Game Info

Stranger Danger is a story-driven visual novel game inspired by Filipino folklore. The game follows Maria Joselina Bashang, who encounter a suspicious stranger that transports her to haunting domain, solving unsettling puzzles in the decrepit domain. Through interactive dialogue choices and branching story paths, players experience suspense, mystery, and lessons about awareness and critical thinking.
